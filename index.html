Our Journey

Company History

From our humble beginnings to becoming an industry leader, our journey is marked by innovation, dedication, and continuous growth.

2001

Foundation

Established with a vision to revolutionize precast concrete manufacturing in India with a small factory in Bangalore.

2007

First Expansion

Expanded operations with a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility and doubled production capacity.

2012

Quality Certification

Achieved ISO 9001 certification, establishing our commitment to quality management systems.

2016

International Projects

Successfully executed major infrastructure projects in Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

2020

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our Green Manufacturing program, reducing carbon footprint by 40% across operations.

2023

Automation Revolution

Implemented AI-driven production systems, increasing efficiency and precision in manufacturing.
